FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions - LUMIN GLASS

1

What types of glass do you offer?

We provide a wide range of premium glass, including tempered, laminated, acoustic, triple-pane, hurricane-resistant, and smart glass systems.

2

Do you handle custom projects?

Yes, all our projects are custom-designed. We work closely with clients, architects, and designers to create tailored solutions.

3

How long does an installation take?

Project timelines depend on size and complexity. Small installations like shower screens can take 1–2 days, while full residential projects may require several weeks.

4

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer free on-site consultations and preliminary quotes for most residential and commercial projects.

5

What warranty do you offer?

Our installations include a 12-month workmanship warranty, while the glass itself carries the manufacturer's warranty (varies by product).

6

Do you work with architects or contractors?

Absolutely. We collaborate with architects, contractors, and interior designers to ensure seamless project execution.

7

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, our 24/7 emergency glass service covers urgent repairs and replacements.

8

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Florida and surrounding regions. For large-scale projects, we may travel upon request.

9

How are payments handled?

A deposit is required to schedule and confirm the project. Final payment is due upon completion. We accept bank transfers, major credit cards, and approved digital payment platforms.

10

Can I get a same-day quote?

For smaller projects, yes. For larger or custom installations, we provide detailed quotes within 24–48 hours after site evaluation.
